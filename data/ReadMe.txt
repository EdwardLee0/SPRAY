Spectral X-ray (SPRAY) Datasets, 1st June 2017.

-------------------
General Description 
-------------------
Numerical thorax phantom and simulated data for spectral X-ray imaging.
 
-------------------
Content 
-------------------  
Five datasets are provided (see table below), which were initially published in [1].
    - thorax_hig_3_cor.mat
	- thorax_med_3_cor.mat
	- thorax_low_3_cor.mat
	- thorax_hig_5_cor.mat
	- thorax_hig_3_sag.mat
	- thorax_hig_5_sag.mat
	
|-------------------------------------------------------------------------
| Phantom Name 		| Conc. (g/cm3)   | Number of materials   | View		
|-------------------------------------------------------------------------
| thorax_hig_3_cor	| 1				  | 3                     | Coronal
| thorax_med_3_cor	| 0.1			  | 3                     | Coronal
| thorax_low_3_cor	| 0.01			  | 3                     | Coronal
| thorax_hig_5_cor	| 1				  | 5                     | Coronal
| thorax_hig_3_sag  | 1				  | 3                     | Sagittal
| thorax_hig_5_sag  | 1				  | 5                     | Sagittal
|-------------------------------------------------------------------------

-------------------
Related datasets and software
-------------------
* The original CT scan of the thorax belongs to the 3D-IRCADb database.
http://www.ircad.fr/research/3dircadb/

* The 3D mass densities of each material were projected in the detector plane using the reconstruction toolkit (RTK).
http://www.openrtk.org/
